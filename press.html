Fact Sheet

About Summit.

Summit Real Estate Services is a fully integrated operator and manager of core-plus commercial real estate. The firm acquires and operates leased commercial properties across the United States — currently 107 assets, 189 tenants, 15 states — primarily on behalf of families, family offices, and private equity sponsors.

Summit's services span acquisition advisory, asset and property management, and operating partnership for institutional sponsors. The firm is led by Kevin McNeil, Principal; Dennis Keane, Director of Operations; and Cody Leivas, Acquisitions.

  • Founded — Operating commercial real estate since the early 2010s
  • Headquarters — Operating across the United States with concentrations in the Midwest
  • Portfolio — 107 commercial assets · 189 tenants · 15 states
  • Investment focus — Core-plus commercial real estate, primarily industrial
  • Clients — Families, family offices, private equity sponsors, GP/co-GP groups
