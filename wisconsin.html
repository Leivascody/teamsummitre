Industrial property management in Wisconsin.

Summit Real Estate Services is the on-the-ground operator for 32 commercial real estate assets across Wisconsin — concentrated in the Milwaukee, Waukesha, and Madison industrial corridors.

Why Wisconsin

One of the most resilient industrial corridors in the country.

Wisconsin's industrial economy — anchored by manufacturing, logistics, and food production — has produced steady tenant demand and durable rent growth across the markets where Summit operates. The Milwaukee MSA and the Madison corridor in particular benefit from a deep base of mid-market manufacturers, established logistics tenants, and a labor force trained in industrial trades.

For private investors and family offices building Midwest industrial portfolios, Wisconsin offers a rare combination: stable, long-tenured tenants; institutional-grade real estate at non-coastal pricing; and a talent pool that's outlasted every recent economic cycle.
