Industrial property management in Missouri.

Summit Real Estate Services is the on-the-ground operator for 31 commercial real estate assets across Missouri — concentrated in St. Louis County and the Earth City / Bridgeton industrial corridor along I-70.

Why Missouri

St. Louis is one of the most active U.S. industrial markets you don't hear enough about.

St. Louis's industrial real estate has quietly outperformed for years — driven by central U.S. logistics geography, a deep manufacturing base, and rent growth that's held up across cycles. Summit's Missouri portfolio runs across the highest-occupancy industrial submarkets: Earth City, Bridgeton, Maryland Heights, Brentwood, and the city itself.

For private investors and PE sponsors, Missouri offers durable yield: long-tenured industrial tenants, replacement-cost-protected pricing, and the kind of operating environment where owner-aligned management compounds returns over a hold period.

What We Do Locally

  • Asset & property management — day-to-day operations across 31 assets, with established Missouri vendor and contractor relationships
