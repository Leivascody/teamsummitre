What We Do

Full-service commercial real estate.

Team Summit is a fully integrated operator and manager of commercial real estate — from acquisition through hold to disposition. We protect and enhance our clients' commercial real estate with institutional discipline.

01 / Acquisitions

Acquisition Advisory

Team Summit helps investors acquire leased commercial properties throughout the United States. Our team is one of the most active in the U.S., and our knowledge, track record, and relationships with the brokerage community position us well to deliver optimal returns for our investors.

Discuss an Acquisition
  • Sourcing — on- and off-market deal flow nationally
  • Valuation — institutional financial underwriting and sensitivity analysis
  • Due Diligence — coordinated physical, environmental, title, and lease audit
  • Strategic Planning — hold thesis, business plan, and exit strategy
  • Negotiation through LOI, PSA, and closing
  • Capital strategy and lender introductions for qualified buyers
02 / Operations

Asset & Property Management

Team Summit provides tailored strategies to meet the specific needs of each owner. Our pro-active approach helps clients realize the full potential of their investment — protecting income, strengthening tenant relationships, and driving consistent performance.

Talk to Operations
  • Lease Administration — abstracts, renewals, options, and CAM tie-out
  • CAM Management & Budgeting — annual budgets, reforecasts, true-ups
  • Expert Tenant Relationships — direct, responsive, retention-focused
  • Vendor & Maintenance Management — proactive PM and capital oversight
  • Owner reporting with institutional-grade clarity and timeliness
  • Insurance, tax appeal coordination, and risk management
03 / Sponsor Support

Private Equity & Sponsor Support

We partner with PE firms and GP groups on deal execution, asset management, and property operations. We act as a force multiplier for lean sponsor teams scaling across the country, with on-the-ground presence in markets where sponsors lack local infrastructure.

Partner with Summit
  • Pre-acquisition diligence and operating budget validation
  • Day-one transition planning and tenant onboarding
  • Asset management plans aligned with the sponsor's hold thesis
  • Quarterly business reviews with LP-ready reporting
  • On-the-ground execution in our 15-state footprint
  • Disposition support — positioning, broker selection, data room curation
04 / Strategy

Portfolio Strategy

Long-term planning for families and investment firms seeking predictable cash flow, disciplined operations, and portfolio growth. Our focus on core-plus real estate produces stable income with selective upside through repositioning.

Build a Strategy
  • Investment thesis and target market mapping
  • Portfolio construction, diversification, and risk parameters
  • Capital deployment pacing and reinvestment strategy
  • Hold-vs-sell analysis and recycling playbook
  • Generational planning support for family ownership
  • Quarterly portfolio reviews and refresh of the long-term plan
