What We Do

Full-service industrial real estate.

From acquisition advisory to hands-on property management, we provide end-to-end solutions for private investors and institutional sponsors building Midwest industrial portfolios.

01 / Acquisition Advisory

Acquisition & Advisory

We guide investors through sourcing, underwriting, due diligence, and negotiation for industrial acquisitions across the Midwest. Our process combines institutional underwriting standards with the discretion and pace of a boutique advisor.

Discuss an Acquisition
  • Off-market and on-market deal sourcing across Midwest industrial corridors
  • Institutional-grade financial underwriting and sensitivity analysis
  • Market and submarket research, comparables, and demand drivers
  • Negotiation strategy through LOI, PSA, and closing
  • Coordinated due diligence: physical, environmental, title, lease audit
  • Capital strategy and lender introductions for qualified buyers
02 / Operations

Property & Asset Management

Hands-on, detail-driven management that protects the asset, strengthens tenant relationships, and drives consistent performance. We treat every building like one we own ourselves.

Talk to Operations
  • Day-to-day property operations and tenant relations
  • Annual budgeting, reforecasting, and NOI-focused performance tracking
  • Vendor management, preventive maintenance, and capital project oversight
  • Lease administration, renewals, and abstract management
  • Owner reporting with institutional-grade clarity and timeliness
  • Insurance, tax appeal coordination, and risk management
03 / Sponsor Support

Private Equity & Sponsor Support

Partnering with PE firms and GP groups on deal execution, asset management, and property operations. We act as a force multiplier for lean sponsor teams scaling across the Midwest.

Partner with Summit
  • Pre-acquisition diligence and operating budget validation
  • Day-one transition planning and tenant onboarding
  • Asset management plans aligned with the sponsor's hold thesis
  • Quarterly business reviews with LP-ready reporting
  • On-the-ground presence in markets where sponsors lack local infrastructure
  • Disposition support: positioning, broker selection, data room curation
04 / Strategy

Portfolio Strategy

Long-term planning for families and investment firms seeking predictable cash flow, disciplined operations, and portfolio growth. We help clients articulate their thesis and execute against it.

Build a Strategy
  • Investment thesis development and target market mapping
  • Portfolio construction, diversification, and risk parameters
  • Capital deployment pacing and reinvestment strategy
  • Hold-vs-sell analysis and recycling playbook
  • Generational planning support for family ownership
  • Quarterly portfolio reviews and refresh of the long-term plan
